# Den Grønne Trepart — Overview

## The political agreement

"Aftale om et Grønt Danmark" was signed June 24, 2024, followed by "Aftale om Implementering af et Grønt Danmark" on November 18, 2024. It is the result of the Green Tripartite negotiations between government, agricultural industry (Landbrug & Fødevarer), and environmental organizations (Danmarks Naturfredningsforening, CONCITO).

The agreement represents the most comprehensive land transformation in Denmark in over 100 years, backed by DKK 43 billion in funding.

## Binding targets

| Target | Quantity | Deadline | Primary measure |
|--------|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Lowland soil withdrawal | 140,000 ha (incl. buffer zones) | 2030 | Rewetting carbon-rich soils |
| Afforestation | 250,000 ha new forest | 2045 | State + private planting |
| Nitrogen reduction (collective) | 13,800 tonnes N/year | 2027+ | Wetlands, mini-wetlands, extraction |
| Watercourse restoration | 7,500 km + 1,500 barrier removals | VP3 period | Remeandering, dam removal |
| Protected nature | 20% of land area | 2030 | Natura 2000, nature national parks |

## Current status (as of Feb 2026)

- **1,059 projects** registered in MARS (236 nitrogen wetlands, 188 lowland, 170 climate-lowland, 255 afforestation)
- **187 hectares** formally completed (vs 140,000 ha target)
- **56,200 hectares** in process (various stages)
- Klimarådet's Feb 2026 report warns that 2030 targets are no longer "anskueliggjort" (substantiated) on current trajectory

## Institutional landscape

- **Ministeriet for Grøn Trepart (MGTP)**: New ministry created 2024, political oversight
- **Styrelsen for Grøn Arealomlægning og Vandmiljø (SGAV)**: Operational agency under MGTP
- **Naturstyrelsen**: Manages ~209,000 ha state land, nature national parks
- **DCE (Aarhus University)**: Scientific monitoring (NOVANA)
- **GEUS**: Geological/groundwater monitoring
- **Danmarks Miljøportal**: Central environmental data platform (state + municipalities + regions)
- **Landbrugsstyrelsen**: Agricultural subsidies and grant administration
- **98 municipalities**: Primary project implementers
- **23 local tripartites**: Decentralized planning units around main catchments

## Funding structure

- DKK 43 billion total
- ~740 million DKK for strategic land acquisition/redistribution
- Multiple subsidy schemes: lowland projects, wetland projects, mini-wetlands, watercourse restoration, afforestation
- Index-regulated reference values set maximum cost-effectiveness per intervention type

## Regulatory context: VP3, the EU Water Framework Directive, and the Green Tripartite

The Green Tripartite did not arise in a vacuum. It builds on (and supersedes) earlier nitrogen reduction commitments:

**Vandområdeplanerne 2021-2027 (VP3)** — Published June 2023. Denmark's implementation of the EU Water Framework Directive (2000/60/EC), which requires all EU member states to achieve "good ecological status" in all water bodies by 2027. VP3 set a nitrogen reduction target of **10,400 tonnes/year**. This would bring roughly 84 of 109 coastal waters into good condition — but full compliance with the directive requires an estimated **~24,000 tonnes/year** reduction.

**Landbrugsaftalen 2021** — The political agricultural agreement from October 2021 ("Aftale om grøn omstilling af dansk landbrug") was the precursor. It committed to roughly the same 10,400 T target but relied heavily on voluntary measures. By 2024, only ~154 T/year had been achieved through voluntary agreements — a conspicuous failure that built political pressure for the tripartite negotiations.

**Den Grønne Trepart 2024** — Raised the nitrogen target from 10,400 to **13,780 tonnes/year** and added binding climate targets (CO2 tax on agriculture), land conversion (140,000 ha lowland), afforestation (250,000 ha), drinking water protection, and biodiversity goals. It is broader and more ambitious than VP3, but the nitrogen component operates through the same operational machinery (MARS, catchment-based planning, local tripartites).

### What this means for the tracker

This tracker focuses on the **Green Tripartite targets** because:

1. The Trepart's nitrogen target (13,780 T) incorporates and exceeds the VP3 target (10,400 T) — same tonnes, same coastal waters, same MARS data
2. The Trepart adds CO2, land conversion, afforestation, and biodiversity goals that VP3 did not cover
3. MARS — our primary data source — tracks implementation of the Trepart, not VP3 separately
4. Reporting VP3 as a separate track would double-count nitrogen progress and confuse users

However, the tracker should provide context that even the Trepart's 13,780 T target falls short of the EU directive's estimated ~24,000 T requirement. As of early 2026, only **5 of 109** Danish coastal waters meet "good ecological status."

## Kommunal klimaindsats og monitorering

Parallelt med den grønne trepart har **Klimaalliancen** (KL + Realdania + CONCITO + C40) etableret et fælles monitoreringsystem for kommunernes klimahandling. 96 af 98 danske kommuner har vedtaget klimahandlingsplaner under DK2020-rammeværket.

### Nøgletal (april 2025-statusrapport)

- Kommunernes samlede udledning: 38 mio. ton CO₂e (2022), ned fra 47 mio. ton i basisåret (2018) — ca. 20% reduktion
- Kommunernes mål: 18,8 mio. ton i 2030 (76% reduktion ift. 1990)
- Lineær fremskrivning: 20,8 mio. ton — kommunerne når ikke samlet de opsatte mål uden acceleration
- Kun 35 af 96 kommuner forventes at nå deres individuelle mål (lineært scenarie)

### CONCITOs 16 omstillingsindikatorer

CONCITO har defineret 16 sektorfordelte omstillingsindikatorer for drivhusgasreduktion på kommuneniveau:
- **Energi (6)**: Bygningsareal, opvarmningskilder, vedvarende energi, biogas, brændsler, CCS/PtX
- **Transport (4)**: Vejtrafikarbejde, modalsplit, køretøjer/drivmidler, infrastruktur
- **Landbrug (3)**: Husdyrproduktion, organiske jorde (lavbund), skov — direkte overlap med vores udtagnings- og skovrejsningssøjler
- **Affald/spildevand (2)**: Affaldsdeponi, spildevand
- **Overordnet (1)**: Samlet drivhusgasudledning pr. kommune

Detaljeret dokumentation: [docs/data-sources/concito-kommunemonitoring.md](data-sources/concito-kommunemonitoring.md)

### Relevans for trackeren

Concitos skov- og lavbundsindikatorer bruger de samme grunddata (MARS) som vores dashboard. Den primære yderligere datakilde er **Energi- og CO₂-regnskabet** (SparEnergi/Ea Energianalyse), som giver kommunefordelt drivhusgasudledning. Denne datakilde er planlagt til integration i forbindelse med kommune-viewet.
